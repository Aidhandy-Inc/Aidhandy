About AidHandy

AidHandy is a premium U.S.-based platform designed to transform air travel by connecting travelers with carefully vetted companions who provide personalized, non-medical support throughout the airport and flight experience.

Our Mission

Why AidHandy Exists

Air travel can be overwhelming—navigating busy airports, managing check-in procedures, dealing with unexpected gate changes, and experiencing in-flight anxiety. While traditional solutions focus on medical care or institutional assistance, many travelers simply need calm, empathetic human support.

AidHandy was created to fill this critical gap by offering a dignified, flexible, and privacy-conscious service that respects traveler independence while providing the reassurance and guidance they need.

Our Services

What We Do

AidHandy connects travelers with professional companions who provide support with:

  • Airport navigation and wayfinding assistance
  • Check-in counter and security checkpoint support
  • Gate location and boarding assistance
  • In-flight reassurance and companionship
  • Luggage handling guidance
  • Connection flight coordination

Important: All services provided through AidHandy are strictly non-medical and non-clinical in nature. We focus on emotional support, navigation assistance, and travel companionship.

Our Values

Our Approach

AidHandy is built on three foundational principles that guide everything we do:

  • Dignity: We believe travelers should remain independent and in control of their journey. Our companions empower rather than enable dependency.
  • Trust: Every companion undergoes rigorous vetting, background checks, and training to meet our professional standards and ensure traveler safety.
  • Privacy: We maintain strict privacy standards. No medical services or sensitive health data are involved in our operations.

Through thoughtful platform design, clear service boundaries, and transparent operations, we create a respectful and premium experience for both travelers and companions.

Our Commitment

Safety & Quality Standards

AidHandy is committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety and service quality:

  • Comprehensive background checks for all companions
  • Ongoing training and professional development
  • 24/7 platform support and monitoring
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Clear service guidelines and boundaries
  • Secure, privacy-first data handling

We're dedicated to creating a marketplace that travelers can trust and companions are proud to be part of.
