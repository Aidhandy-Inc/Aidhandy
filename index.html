How It Works

A seamless experience for everyone

Whether you're seeking support or offering it, AidHandy provides a trusted platform built on empathy, safety, and reliability.

For Travelers

Book on-demand support for airport check-in, gate navigation, boarding assistance, and in-flight reassurance. Whether you're anxious about flying, need mobility support, or simply want peace of mind, our vetted companions are here to help make your journey stress-free.

For Companions

Join our elite network of travel companions and make a meaningful impact on people's journeys. Set your own schedule, earn competitive compensation, and help travelers feel supported. We handle all payments, insurance, notifications, and logistics—you bring empathy and reliability.
